About Us

who we are

Malsof ,short for Malawi Software, is a web development and graphic design startup aimed at professionalizing businesses and institutions, and improving how they offer their services to their clients and customers. This means making their services and their business base well-presented and attractive as well as their internal operations easier to manage and execute.

our belief

We believe that businesses should be conducted in a professional manner so as to legitimize your services and your business or institution as a whole. This will not only attract customers but also attract potential investors and partners
